About Vangalamman UPVS

Building a legacy of quality and trust in pipe manufacturing and construction materials

Our Story

Founded over 25 years ago, Vangalamman UPVS began with a simple vision: to provide the construction industry with the highest quality pipes and materials that builders and contractors could rely on.

From our humble beginnings as a small manufacturing unit, we've grown into one of the region's most trusted names in pipe manufacturing and construction material supply. Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and an unrelenting focus on customer satisfaction.

Today, we serve thousands of clients across multiple states, from individual contractors to large-scale infrastructure projects, maintaining the same dedication to excellence that defined our founding principles.

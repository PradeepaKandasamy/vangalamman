Our Services

Premium UPVC, Wooden, Foam Board & WPC Solutions for Modern Spaces

At Vangalamman UPVS, we deliver high-quality door and window solutions designed for durability, aesthetics, and long-term performance.

🪟

UPVC Window & Door Systems

Our UPVC window and door systems are engineered for superior insulation, durability, and low maintenance. Ideal for modern residential and commercial buildings.

  • Casement, sliding & tilt-and-turn systems
  • Excellent thermal & sound insulation
  • Weatherproof & corrosion resistant
  • Custom sizes and color options
🚪

Premium Wooden Doors

Crafted with precision and elegance, our wooden doors enhance the beauty of interiors while offering lasting strength and durability.

  • Main doors & interior wooden doors
  • High-quality seasoned wood
  • Custom designs & finishing
  • Natural aesthetics & durability
📋

Foam Boards

Our foam boards are lightweight, moisture-resistant, and ideal for interiors, partitions, signage, and modular applications.

  • Lightweight yet strong panels
  • Termite & moisture resistant
  • Easy installation and handling
  • Perfect for interiors & displays
🧱

WPC Doors & Frames

WPC doors and frames are a modern alternative to wood, offering water resistance, durability, and eco-friendly performance.

  • 100% waterproof & termite proof
  • Strong, long-lasting material
  • Ideal for kitchens & bathrooms
  • Multiple finishes available
🔧

Installation & After-Sales Support

We provide professional installation and dependable after-sales support to ensure complete customer satisfaction.

  • Expert on-site installation
  • Accurate measurement & fitting
  • Maintenance & warranty support
  • Responsive customer service
